Rarebit Project Home

Rarebit is a protocol designed to enable collectible digital works to be issued and traded on the bitcoin network. Using a Rarebit client, an artist or author can issue limited editions of digital content online, and collectors can trade units of the editions with the assurance they are authentic.

Rarebit works by associating the hash of a digital file with a small amount of bitcoin value, which can be transferred indefinitely while remaining provably bound to the content it represents and to the author that originated it.

NEW JUNE 2014 *Web1* client (alpha)

A full client that runs in the browser. Loads, hashes, issues editions, and transfers lots of digital works. Alpha test version. In this implementation, issue transactions carry the author's signature and thus serve as timestamped certificates of authenticity.
